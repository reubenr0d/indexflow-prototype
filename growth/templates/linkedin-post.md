## Format Note

LinkedIn algorithm favors posts with **1200-1600 characters** (not words). Use **single-line paragraphs** separated by blank lines for mobile readability -- LinkedIn's mobile app crops dense paragraphs. For carousel posts, include slide content below the main post body.

## Notes

- No hashtag walls. Use 3-5 relevant hashtags max at the end: #DeFi #StructuredProducts #Web3 #Fintech #Blockchain
- Tag relevant people or companies when it adds value (not spam tagging).
- LinkedIn posts with images get ~2x engagement. Attach a diagram, chart, or clean screenshot.
- Best posting times: Tue-Thu, 08:00-10:00 in your target audience's timezone.
- Regulatory and institutional content outperforms on LinkedIn compared to all other channels.
