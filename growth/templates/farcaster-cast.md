## Notes

- Farcaster audience skews technical and VC-heavy. Lead with mechanism design, architecture decisions, or data -- not marketing copy.
- Cast in relevant channels: `/defi` for protocol content, `/build` for engineering posts, `/ethereum` for ecosystem takes.
- Frames (embedded apps) get high engagement if you can link to testnet or an interactive demo.
- Best engagement: weekday mornings US time (14:00-17:00 UTC).
- Reply to other builders' casts with genuine technical commentary to build presence before pushing your own content.
- Avoid: threads (Farcaster isn't threaded like X), hashtags, exclamation points, emojis in every sentence.
