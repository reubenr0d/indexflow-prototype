## Notes

- Personalize the first sentence for every show. Generic pitches get ignored.
- Keep the initial email under 250 words (excluding the angle options -- pick only one).
- Attach nothing to the first email. Offer to share materials if they're interested.
- Track all outreach in a spreadsheet or in this file's Status field.
- Best response rates: send Tue-Thu morning in the host's timezone.
